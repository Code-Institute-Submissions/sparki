The Research

Below are the list of all of the companies I have researched for this project. I have included an explaination of why I researched this company and have written pros & cons for some of the features on their website. This page has inspired my mockups that I drew out, click here to view the mockup page.

SM Electrical page via Checkatrade website

Checkatrade is a well known company who specialise in tradesmen in fields like Electricians. A lot of customers rely on this website so this would be a good competitor. I chose to look at SM Electrical because it is a company based in the surrey area.

Pros

  • The first thing your eyes are drawn to are the display of the reviews which is a line graph with ratings for reliability & timekeeping, tidiness, courtesy, workmanship and invoice history.
  • Icons are very simple
  • Displays all of the services
  • The home page sums up all of the pages.
  • Works well in different views (desktop, mobile, medium, etc.).
  • Navigation pages are simple and layed out well.

Cons

  • When hovering over the navigation bar, there is not a lot of animation or change in colour.
  • The home page seems quite squashed up.
Ross Hayes Electrical

This is an electrician company based in the Berkshire area. I chose this company as this was part of the location Sparki is based. This is one of the companies that Sparki is competing with.

Pros

  • When scrolling down, the background images change. Images are of work they have done.
  • The home page is spread out well. Nothing is squashed up.
  • Colour scheme worked well.
  • On the service page, it shows a Get In Touch button underneath each section.
  • Works well on all viewports.

Cons

  • Displays of the images were a bit too big. A lot of scrolling to view the next bit of information.
  • Some of the text is too big for some of the sections.
Ascot Electrical Services LTD

Like the previous company, this company is based in the Berkshire area. This is another competitior of Sparki.

Pros

  • Simple layout
  • Good colour scheme

Cons

  • Does not work in medium or mobile views.
  • Not a lot of pictures on the pages (apart from on gallery).
  • The blocks for each section on the pages are a bit too big.
  • Text on the pages are bigger than the navigation bar text.
The Handy Squad

I chose this electrcian company as this company is online based. There website has information for customers and navigates customers to their contact page.

Pros

  • The colour scheme works well
  • Information is laid out well. Very chear.
  • They have used icons for different services.

Cons

  • The navigation bar is a bit too busy
